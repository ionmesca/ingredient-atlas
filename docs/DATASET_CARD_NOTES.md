# Dataset Card Notes

The Hugging Face dataset card should stay short and practical.

Must include:

- what Ingredient Atlas is;
- who it is for;
- dataset structure;
- CC0-1.0 direction;
- AI image disclosure;
- nutrition caution;
- contact email;
- relationship to Beets;
- known limitations.

Avoid:

- claiming every image is correct;
- claiming nutrition is medical-grade;
- internal source details;
- private review artifacts;
- hype.
