# Launch Playbook

The goal is not a loud launch. The goal is to get Ingredient Atlas in front of people who are already building recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning tools.

## Best Sequence

1. Make Hugging Face public.
2. Make GitHub public.
3. Publish npm.
4. Post Show HN.
5. Post r/datasets.
6. Post a short Hugging Face community note.
7. Send 10 direct notes to maintainers.
8. Wait, reply, fix issues.
9. Send another 10 notes only if the first batch gets useful replies.

## What To Say

- I made this because ingredient images are annoying to source cleanly.
- It is CC0 direction.
- It is useful for recipe apps and pantry tools.
- It is AI-generated and reviewed, but not perfect.
- Corrections are welcome.

## What Not To Say

- do not call it perfect;
- do not overclaim nutrition accuracy;
- do not hide AI generation;
- do not ask for stars;
- do not post the same copy everywhere.

## What Usually Fails

- one generic launch post;
- posting in too many places at once;
- asking for upvotes;
- making people guess how to use the data;
- no demo;
- no clear correction path.

## What Usually Works

- a useful demo;
- clear screenshots;
- direct notes to the right builders;
- quick replies to corrections;
- small follow-up releases that show the project is alive.
