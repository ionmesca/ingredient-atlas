Hi,

I made Ingredient Atlas, an open dataset of ingredient images and metadata for recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps.

It includes 1,673 ingredient records and 5,019 image files, with WebP thumbnails, PNG fallbacks, slugs, aliases, categories, checksums, JSONL, Parquet, and a public-safe manifest. The images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. Metadata and AI-generated images are released under CC0-1.0.
