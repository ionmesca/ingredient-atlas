Hi,

I saw your project and thought this might be useful.

I made Ingredient Atlas, an open dataset of ingredient images and metadata for recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps.

It has 1,673 ingredient records, 5,019 image files, slugs, aliases, categories, checksums, and a small npm resolver.

The images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. The point is to save app builders from having to source every ingredient thumbnail manually.

Dataset: https://huggingface.co/datasets/ionicam/ingredient-atlas
Repo: https://github.com/ionmesca/ingredient-atlas
