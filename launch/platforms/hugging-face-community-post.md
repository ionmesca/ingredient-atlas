Title:

Ingredient Atlas: open ingredient images and metadata for recipe apps

Body:

Ingredient Atlas is a public dataset of ingredient images and metadata for people building recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps.

It includes 1,673 ingredient records, 5,019 image files, JSONL, Parquet, checksums, aliases, categories, and review status.

The images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. The dataset is not meant to be a perfect recognition benchmark. It is meant to be useful as app data.

I would love feedback on the dataset card, metadata fields, missing aliases, and any ingredient images that look wrong.

Dataset: https://huggingface.co/datasets/ionicam/ingredient-atlas
Repo: https://github.com/ionmesca/ingredient-atlas
