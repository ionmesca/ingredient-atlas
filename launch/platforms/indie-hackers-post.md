Title:

I made an open ingredient image dataset for recipe app builders

Body:

I kept needing clean ingredient images for food software work, so I packaged the dataset as Ingredient Atlas.

It is not a startup product. It is a useful pile of app data:

- 1,673 ingredient records
- 5,019 images
- slugs, aliases, categories, checksums
- Hugging Face files
- small npm resolver

Images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis.

If you are building a recipe, grocery, pantry, or meal-planning app, I would love to know whether this shape is useful.
