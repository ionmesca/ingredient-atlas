I made Ingredient Atlas, an open ingredient image and metadata dataset for people building recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps.

The v0 includes:

- 1,673 ingredient records
- 5,019 image files
- WebP thumbnails and PNG fallbacks
- stable slugs, aliases, categories, checksums, and review status
- JSONL, Parquet, Hugging Face files, and a small npm resolver

Most food datasets are for dish classification, packaged products, or research. This is meant to be app data.

Images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. Nutrition metadata is best-effort and should not be used as medical or dietary advice.

I would love feedback from anyone building food software.
