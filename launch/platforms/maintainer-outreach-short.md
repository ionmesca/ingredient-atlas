Hi, I made Ingredient Atlas and thought it might be useful for this project.

It is an open ingredient image and metadata dataset for recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps. It has 1,673 ingredient records, 5,019 image files, slugs, aliases, categories, and checksums.

Images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. Corrections are welcome.
