Name:

Ingredient Atlas

Tagline:

Open ingredient images and metadata for recipe apps

Description:

Ingredient Atlas is a CC0-oriented dataset of clean ingredient images, slugs, aliases, categories, checksums, and app-ready metadata. It is useful for recipe, grocery, pantry, and meal-planning apps.

Maker comment:

I made Ingredient Atlas because recipe apps need ingredient images, but most food datasets are built for dishes, product labels, or model training.

This v0 has 1,673 ingredient records and 5,019 image files. Images are AI-generated and reviewed on a best-effort basis. The goal is to make a useful baseline that people can correct and extend.
